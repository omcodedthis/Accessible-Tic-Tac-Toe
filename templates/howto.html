How to Play?

The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. Players take turns putting their marks ('O' or 'X') in empty squares. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. You can also save the board after a game by clicking the "Save Board" button which will autofill the details for you.
