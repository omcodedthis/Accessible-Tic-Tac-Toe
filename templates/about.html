About Tic-Tac-Toe:

While most games focus on graphics and animations, Tic-Tac-Toe is inclusive to Persons With Disabilities (PWDs), specifically persons with sensory impairments. Persons with sensory impairments commonly use a screen reader as an interface to navigate the web and Tic-Tac-Toe has been created with this in mind. Please do note that this is currently a prototype.
